AR System


About the project
Team final project for KEA's Datamatiker AP. Grade: excellent

Together with two colleagues we combined Scrum and Design thinking for our process and developed an augmented reality android tourist guide
and a supporting administration system.

Nov 2018

Prototype Development


About the project
A multidisciplinary team of 2 multimedia designers and 2 developers. We developed an AR solution for this local guided-tours company.
Our goal was to "Bring Stories Alive" as well as to add a competitive advantage
to the company's SoMe presence.
The process
We went through a 4-day course on Unity 3D and 1 day on Ideation. We worked with Scrum. An experienced tutor guided us while practising design methods from the Amsterdam MediaLab's Toolkit .
We researched about the target group of the company, and through ideation tecniques we developed a series of prototypes.
After iterative user testing we ended up with a working AR mobile application that we pitched to the client during an open event.
